About ministry of power

The Ministry of Power started functioning independently with effect from 2nd july,1992.
Earlier it was known as ministry of Energy
sources.Electricity is a concurrent subject
at Entry 38 in List III of the seventh schedule
of the Constitution of India.The Ministry of
Power is primarily responsible for the
development of electrical energy in the
country.
